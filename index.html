BilPost

Bilingual content publishing for creators.

BilPost is a personal content scheduling and cross-posting tool built for a bilingual (English/Spanish) short-form video creator. It allows the creator to upload their own original videos once and publish them, with appropriate captions and hashtags, across their own authorized social media accounts.

What BilPost does

The creator connects their own TikTok, Instagram, and Facebook accounts via each platform's official authorization flow. Once connected, BilPost publishes video content the creator has already produced to the accounts they've authorized, on their own behalf.

What BilPost does not do

BilPost does not access, store, or share data belonging to any user other than the authenticated account owner. It does not post on behalf of any account that has not explicitly authorized it via OAuth.
