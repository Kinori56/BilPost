Data Deletion Instructions

BilPost only stores the minimum data required to publish content to accounts you have explicitly authorized: an account access token and a record of what was posted and when. No other personal data is collected.

How to delete your data

You can remove BilPost's access and request deletion of any associated data at any time in either of these ways:

  • Revoke access directly: Go to Instagram or Facebook Settings > Apps and Websites (or Business Integrations), find BilPost, and remove it. This immediately revokes BilPost's access token.
  • Request manual deletion: Contact the app owner via the email listed in the developer application, requesting deletion of any stored data associated with your account. Requests are processed within 30 days.

Once access is revoked or a deletion request is processed, any stored access tokens and posting records associated with your account are permanently deleted.
