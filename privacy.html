Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how BilPost ("we," "us," or "the app") handles information when you use our service to publish content to your own connected social media accounts.

Who this applies to

BilPost is a personal-use tool operated by its owner to manage their own content publishing across their own authorized TikTok, Instagram, and Facebook accounts. It is not a public-facing service offered to third parties.

Information we access

When you authorize BilPost via a platform's official OAuth flow, we access only the minimum information required to publish content on your behalf, including:

  • Basic account identification (to confirm which account is authorized)
  • Permission to publish video content to that account

We do not access private messages, contact lists, or any data unrelated to publishing the content you have authorized.

Information we store

We store the minimum data required to operate: account authorization tokens (so we can publish on your behalf without asking you to re-authenticate every time), and records of what was posted and when.

How we use information

Information is used exclusively to publish the content you provide to the accounts you have authorized. We do not use your data for advertising, and we do not build behavioral profiles.

Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties. Data is only transmitted to the social media platforms (TikTok, Instagram, Facebook) as required to publish your authorized content.

Data retention and deletion

You may revoke BilPost's access to your account at any time through the relevant platform's app permissions settings (e.g. TikTok Settings > Apps, or Facebook/Instagram > Business Integrations). Upon revocation, we delete any stored access tokens for that account.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be directed to the app owner through the contact information provided in the developer application.
